Entrance Exams > JEE Main - Entrance Exams

Yes, JEE Main 2019 Syllabus is usually similar to that of CBSE Class 12th Board or any board following NCERT books. The questions are vastly from NCERT. So, if the student is preparing for the board exam, it is advised to keep your concepts clear, it will further help you in various JEE Entrance Examination. Most of the engineering entrance examination covers the syllabus of Class 11th and 12th. The content of the JEE Main Syllabus 2019 may slightly differ from State Board but if you are well prepared for your board exams, cracking the JEE Mains 2019 wouldn’t be much difficult.

.

With the creation of NTA, various changes were expected. But, only the mode of examination changes when it comes to the exam pattern. The syllabus and marks distribution remains the same. The examination will now be conducted twice a year instead of just once a year.

Let’s once have a look at the tentative schedule for JEE Main 2019

JEE Main 2019 January Exam

Dates of Examination

Between 6 – 20 Jan 2019(8 sittings, candidates can choose any one)

JEE Main 2019 April Exam

Dates of Examination

Between 7 – 21 Apr 2019(8 sittings, candidates can choose any one)
