NEET 2019 Syllabus: NEET Exam Syllabus For Biology Physics, And Chemistry

 

NEET 2019 Syllabus: The newly-established exam conducting authority (National Testing Agency) will conduct the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test two times a year (February and May). While NTA will conduct and declare the result, the syllabus is prepared by the Medical Council of India (MCI). The Medical Council of India prepares the syllabus for the NEET exam after reviewing the curriculum of Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Council Board of School Education (COBSE) and National Council of Education Research and Training (NCERT). So, it is vital to get hold of the complete NTA NEET 2019 exam syllabus right from the beginning and be well-versed in it.

NEET 2019 Syllabus Structure:

With the announcement of tentative dates for the NEET 2019 exams earlier this month, all the medical aspirants who have cleared or are appearing in their Class 12th exam in 2018 have already got on to their toes. For undergraduate medical and dental courses in all private and public colleges, NEET score is mandatory. If you are eyeing for the NEET exam 2019, you should start the preparation right away.
